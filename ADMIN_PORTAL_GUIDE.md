## Getting Started

### Accessing the Admin Portal

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the admin portal URL
2. You will see the login page

### Login Credentials

- **Email:** admin@zenyourlife.be
- **Password:** admin12345

### Logging In

1. Enter your email address in the "Username or Email" field
2. Enter your password in the "Password" field
3. Click the "Log In" button
4. You will be redirected to the Dashboard

### Logging Out

1. Click the "Sign Out" button at the bottom of the sidebar
2. You will be redirected to the login page

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is your central hub for monitoring business performance at a glance.

|---------|-------------|

## Massage Appointments

This section manages all massage and wellness service bookings.

### Viewing Appointments

1. Navigate to **Massage Appointments** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a list of all bookings on the left panel
3. Click any booking to view full details on the right panel

### Filtering Appointments

Use the filter tabs at the top of the booking list:

| Filter | Shows |
|--------|-------|
| **All** | All appointments regardless of status |
| **Pending** | Appointments awaiting confirmation |
| **Confirmed** | Approved appointments |
| **Done** | Completed appointments |

### Searching Appointments

- Use the search bar to find appointments by:
  - Customer name
  - Appointment ID
  - Service name
  - Email address

### Creating a New Appointment

1. Click the **"New Appointment"** button (top right)
2. Fill in the customer information:
   - First Name & Last Name
   - Email & Phone
   - Country
   - Gender
3. Select a **Service** from the dropdown
4. Choose **Date** and **Time**
5. Add any **Special Requests** or **Messages** (optional)
6. Click **"Create Appointment"**

### Managing Appointment Status

When viewing an appointment, you can:

| Action | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Confirm Appointment** | Approve a pending booking |
| **Mark Complete** | When the service has been delivered |
| **Send Message** | Contact the customer |
| **Cancel** | Cancel an appointment |
| **Delete** | Permanently remove the appointment |

### Appointment Details View

The right panel shows complete information:
- Customer name and contact details
- Appointment date, time, and day
- Current status
- Service booked
- Timeline (created & last updated dates)
- Special requests and messages

## Booking Management

This section controls your availability and scheduling settings.

### Managing Time Slots

1. Navigate to **Booking Management** in the sidebar
2. Configure which time slots are available for each day

### Blocking Dates

To block specific dates when you're unavailable:

1. Go to Booking Management
2. Select the date(s) you want to block
3. Choose to block:
   - **Full Day** - No bookings allowed for the entire day
   - **Specific Time Slots** - Block only certain hours

### Setting Weekly Schedule

Configure your regular working hours:

1. Set working/non-working days (e.g., closed on weekends)
2. Define available time slots for each working day
3. Save your schedule

**Note:** Saturdays and Sundays can be set as non-working days, and they will automatically show as unavailable on the customer booking page.

## Services

Manage the massage and wellness services offered.

### Viewing Services

1. Navigate to **Services** in the sidebar
2. See all available services

### Service Information

Each service displays:
- Service name/title
- Description
- Duration (in minutes)
- Price (in EUR)
- Category
- Active/Inactive status

### Adding a New Service

1. Click **"Add Service"** or **"New Service"** button
2. Fill in:
   - Service Title
   - Description
   - Duration
   - Price
   - Category
3. Set status to Active
4. Save the service

### Editing a Service

1. Click on the service you want to edit
2. Modify the details as needed
3. Save changes

### Deactivating a Service

- Toggle the service status to "Inactive"
- Inactive services won't appear on the customer booking page

## Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Verify you're using the correct email and password
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Make sure Caps Lock is off

**Appointments not showing?**
- Refresh the page
- Check your filter settings (ensure "All" is selected)
- Try the search function

**Time slots not appearing for customers?**
- Check Booking Management settings
- Ensure the day is set as a working day
- Verify time slots are configured

**Emails not being sent?**
- Check the server is running
- Verify email configuration in backend settings

### Getting Help

For technical support or questions:
- Contact your system administrator
- Report issues at: https://github.com/Synquic/zenyourlife/issues
